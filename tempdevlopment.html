What Is Calisthenics ?

Most of us think the only way get fit is by spending hours at the gym. I wouldn't blame you for thinking that way., but what if I told you there was another way you can not only get fit but also learn to control your body.

Calisthenics is a form of fitness in which we use gravity and our body weight to workout. From losing weight, building Strength/endurance to learning mind blowing skills like Handstand and humanflag, Calisthenics not only helps you build a good physique but also a strong sense of discipline and will power.
